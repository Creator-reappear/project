Now lets learn about the above tags in detail...!
  • {html}
    • It is main part as it holds the entire document. Its the starting tag
  • {/html}
    • It is ending tag of the {html}
  • {head}
    • It is the starting of the {head}
  • {/head}
    • it is the ending of the {head} tag
  • {title}
    • it is the starting of the {title} tag
  • {/title}
    • It is the ending of the {title} tag
  • {body}
    • It holds many tags in it.It is the starting of {body} tag
  • {/body}
    • It is the ending of the {body} tag
